Quotation - Enterprise Real Estate Management System

1. Project Overview

This document outlines a complete Real Estate ERP solution tailored for builders and developers. It covers every core operation: from pre-sales and customer lifecycle management to project progress tracking, document management, procurement, and finance. Each module is built with usability, scalability, and security at its core—ensuring digital transformation for real estate firms.

2. Module-wise Breakdown & Estimated Effort

ModuleDetailed DescriptionEst. Effort (Hours)
Dashboard & BI Comprehensive BI dashboards featuring real-time data visualizations, customizable metrics, heat maps of site progress, sales funnels, financial KPIs, and more. Enables CXOs to make proactive, data-driven decisions. 32 hrs
Project Management Handles multiple real estate projects with phase-wise planning, task scheduling, RERA compliance, unit grouping (towers, plots), milestone configuration, and work progress mapping with on-site reporting. 45 hrs
Sales & CRM Lead funnel automation with follow-up tracking, team-wise lead allocation, agent commission mapping, and personalized follow-ups via WhatsApp/SMS. Customizable statuses and campaign analytics. 40 hrs

Finance Covers real-time billing, GST tax invoices, project ledger tracking, financial reporting, payment due alerts, aging reports, and Tally-compatible exports. Secured multi-level approval workflow for transactions. 39 hrs
Security & Roles Robust RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) system with department-level restrictions, audit logs, multi-device login detection, IP filtering, and support for two-factor authentication (2FA). 20 hrs
