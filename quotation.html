Quotation - Enterprise Real Estate Management System

1. Project Overview

This document outlines a complete Real Estate ERP solution tailored for builders and developers. It covers every core operation: from pre-sales and customer lifecycle management to project progress tracking, document management, procurement, and finance. Each module is built with usability, scalability, and security at its core—ensuring digital transformation for real estate firms.

ModuleDetailed DescriptionEst. Effort (Hours)
Dashboard & BI Advanced business intelligence dashboard with role-based data visualization, real-time KPIs for project progress, lead funnel analysis, collection vs expense, user productivity metrics, and download-ready MIS reports for top management. 60 hrs
